# Task Description

Document the Agile Manifesto and its 12 principles. Four values: Individuals and interactions over processes and tools, Working software over comprehensive documentation, Customer collaboration over contract negotiation, Responding to change over following a plan. Twelve principles summarized with personal interpretation for each. Compare Agile vs Waterfall: iterative vs sequential, flexible vs rigid, customer involvement, delivery frequency, change handling. Include scenarios where each approach fits best.
